Frontend Roadmap

Frontend is probably the best and most welcoming direction to enter IT. But it is not so easy with him - it is easy to start in the frontend and difficult to become a master. It will not have the pain of choosing a programming language, but there will be a struggle with its shortcomings. There are a lot of tools in this area, the study of each of which draws on the development of a separate profession.
